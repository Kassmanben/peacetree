Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if there is an emergency? Call 911 right away.

Q: How long will I have to wait to set up an appointment? Typically an appointment can be made within a couple days.

Q: How many sessions will I need? Each person is different. The initial consultation will help you to get a feel for how to best work toward meeting your therapeutic goals.

Q: How often will we meet? Clients typically come for sessions weekly or every other week, depending upon individual needs. Sessions last 60 minutes each, unless otherwise arranged ahead of time.

Q: Where do we meet? I travel to you. People are often more comfortable and productive when they choose where to meet. Options from past clients include: home/office/workplace, coffee shops/restaurants, libraries, parks, going on walks and even shooting hoops on the basketball court. If the location is safe, conducive to a therapy session and reasonably accessible, I can be flexible.

Q: How much will it cost and how do I pay? A reasonable sliding fee scale is available based on need. Payments can be cash or check. I do not accept insurance. For more info, see my Services page.

Q: What are your hours? Monday through Friday 8:30 a.m. - 7:00 p.m., Saturdays 8:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.

Q: Do you accept insurance, Medicare or Medicaid? I do not. However, I offer a sliding fee to ALL clients (more info on Services page).

Q: How do I begin? It is important that you find someone with whom you feel comfortable and who meets your criteria. Contact me for a FREE initial consultation. It gives you a chance to ask questions as well as provide you with a sense of who I am. If I am not the best person to work with you, I will do my best to offer appropriate referrals to other professionals.
